Lasagna

Description

This recipe will show you how to produce delicious italian style lasagna.

Ingredients

  • 400g Ground beef
  • 400g Chopped tomatos
  • 10 Lasagna pasta plates
  • 50g Of your favorite cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Cooka de beefa
  2. Boila de pasta
  3. Enjoya de lasagna
